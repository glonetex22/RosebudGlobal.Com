Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weight capacity of the tray table?

The tray table can safely hold up to 15 kg (33 lbs) when properly assembled. We recommend distributing weight evenly across the surface.

Is the tray removable?

Yes! The tray top is completely removable, making it perfect for serving snacks and drinks. Simply lift it off the base when needed.

How do I clean the table?

Wipe clean with a damp cloth. For stubborn stains, use mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners that may damage the powder-coated finish.
